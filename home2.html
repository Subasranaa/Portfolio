Research Objective

Accurately forecasting the pandemic spread is crucial for implementing effective measures to control its transmission and minimize its impact. My research focuses on multivariate time-series forecasting using machine learning and statistical techniques on the pandemic dataset, with a primary focus on predicting COVID-19 mortality rates within the United States at both national and state levels.
