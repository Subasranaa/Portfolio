Research Objective

Accurately forecasting the pandemic spread is crucial for implementing effective measures to control its transmission and minimize its impact. My research focuses on Spatiotemporal Forecasting using Deep Learning on the pandemic dataset, with a primary focus on predicting COVID-19 mortality rates within the United States at both national and state levels.
